Hospital Inventory

Medicine catalog and stock visibility on native Odoo Inventory

Hospital Inventory is a thin clinical layer over Odoo's native Inventory app. It adds medicine-specific fields to the product catalog, expiry tracking to stock lots, and a dashboard that aggregates on-hand quantity, reorder thresholds, and batch expiry -- all in PostgreSQL, not looped in Python.

Key Capabilities

  • Clinical fields on the product catalog: generic name, dosage form, strength, prescription/controlled-substance flags, and a reorder threshold.
  • Expiry-date tracking on stock lots/serials, with live (non-stored) expiring-soon and expired flags.
  • An Inventory Dashboard: KPI cards for low stock and expiring/expired batches, plus a drill-down table, backed entirely by a SQL view.
  • Daily automated alerts: expiry reminders within 30 days, and low-stock reminders against the reorder threshold, both as Activities.
  • A dedicated Pharmacy warehouse and stock location, and a Hospital Medicines product category.
  • A Low Stock QWeb report.

Built for the Full Suite

Hospital Inventory depends only on ``hospital_base`` and Odoo's native ``stock`` app -- deliberately not ``hospital_doctor`` -- so that the future ``hospital_pharmacy`` module can depend on both ``hospital_doctor`` and ``hospital_inventory`` together without creating a dependency cycle. A medicine is a real ``product.product``, so stock, valuation, and purchasing stay 100% native Odoo Inventory.
