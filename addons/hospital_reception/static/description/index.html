Hospital Reception

Front-desk operations for the Hospital Management System

Hospital Reception is the front-desk addon of the Hospital Management System suite, built on ``hospital_base``. It gives reception staff a fast registration flow, appointment booking and check-in, a live reception dashboard, and a printable queue ticket -- everything needed to get a patient from "just walked in" to "waiting for the nurse" in seconds.

Key Capabilities

  • Combined search-existing-or-register-new patient flow with a non-blocking duplicate-match warning.
  • Appointment booking with calendar, list and form views, and one-click check-in that creates and confirms the visit.
  • A live Reception Dashboard: KPI strip, doctor-grouped queue, and today's appointments.
  • A mandatory-reason visit cancellation wizard.
  • A printable, thermal-printer-friendly queue ticket / token slip.

Built for the Full Suite

Hospital Reception extends ``hospital_base``'s patient and visit models via standard Odoo inheritance and reuses its duplicate detection service, sequencing, and security groups -- keeping the whole hospital_* suite consistent and upgrade-safe.
